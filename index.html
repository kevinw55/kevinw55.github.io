    Web Development

    These lectures are an introduction to HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The presentations are in Powerpoint format, and the exercises are Word documents. Be aware that they were last revised in 2015, and so are a little out of date with regard to the latest developments in website design. However, you may find them useful as an introduction to the topic.
